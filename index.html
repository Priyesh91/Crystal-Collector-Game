Crystal-Game




Make the magic number hit EXACTLY ZERO by clicking on the crystals.

Rules:
1.) Crystals will have a hidden value at the start of each game.
2.) Click the crystal to find out how much is taken away from the magic number.
3.) Click the crystals in various combinations to make the number reach EXACTLY ZERO!
4.) You Win if you hit zero but if you go past zero you loose!
